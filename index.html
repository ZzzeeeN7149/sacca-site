About Us

Steel Fabrication & Site Welding

Sacca Engineering is a steel fabrication and site welding business based at Golden Grove, South Australia, working on residential and construction projects throughout the greater Adelaide area.

From structural steel frames for new builds to custom fabrication work, every job is built to fit and finished on site.

  • Structural steel fabrication for new builds
  • On-site steel installation and site welding
  • Custom fabrication: gates, frames & outdoor steel features
